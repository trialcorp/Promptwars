What is VenuePulse?

VenuePulse is an AI-powered crowd intelligence platform designed for live sporting events and large arenas.

Every attendee acts as a sensor, reporting what they see on the ground—long queues, empty sections, great food stalls, parking chaos, or sudden weather changes. Gemini AI aggregates, translates, and analyzes these individual reports to create actionable, real-time guidance for everyone.

Think of it as "Waze for stadiums" — powered by the crowd, refined by AI, helping everyone have a better experience.
