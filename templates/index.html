What is VenueFlow?

VenueFlow is a crowd-sourced intelligence platform for live sporting events. Every attendee can report what they see — long queues, empty sections, great food stalls, parking chaos, weather changes. Gemini AI analyzes these reports and creates actionable guidance for everyone.

Think of it as "Waze for stadiums" — powered by the crowd, refined by AI, helping everyone have a better experience.
